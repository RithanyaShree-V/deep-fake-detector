About DeepFake Detector

An AI-powered solution for detecting manipulated media and protecting against misinformation.

Our Technology

Deep Learning

We use state-of-the-art EfficientNet-based neural networks trained to detect subtle manipulation artifacts in images and videos.

Face Detection

MTCNN face detection identifies and extracts facial regions for focused analysis of potential deepfake manipulations.

Explainability

Grad-CAM visualizations show exactly which regions of the image influenced the AI's decision, making our results interpretable.

Video Analysis

Frame sampling and batch processing enable efficient analysis of video content with frame-by-frame breakdown.

What We Detect

Deepfake Faces

Face swaps, facial reenactment, and other AI-based face manipulation techniques used in deepfake videos.

  • Face swapping (FaceSwap, DeepFaceLab)
  • Facial reenactment
  • Lip sync manipulation
  • Expression transfer

AI-Generated Images

Images created entirely by generative AI models including GANs and diffusion models.

  • GAN-generated faces (StyleGAN, etc.)
  • Diffusion model outputs (DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion)
  • AI art generators
  • Neural style transfers

How It Works

1

Upload

Upload your image or video file through our secure interface. We accept JPG, PNG, WEBP images and MP4, AVI, MOV videos.

2

Preprocessing

The media is preprocessed, faces are detected and extracted, and frames are sampled for video content.

3

AI Analysis

Multiple neural networks analyze the content for deepfake signatures and AI-generation artifacts.

4

Results

Receive detailed results with confidence scores, visual explanations, and a downloadable PDF report.

Ethical Use & Disclaimer

Important Notice

This tool provides probabilistic assessments based on AI analysis. Results should not be considered legally definitive or used as the sole basis for important decisions.

Responsible Use Guidelines

DO

  • Use for detecting potential misinformation
  • Verify media authenticity before sharing
  • Use as one tool among multiple verification methods
  • Report findings responsibly
  • Respect privacy and consent laws

DON'T

  • Use results as legal evidence without expert verification
  • Make accusations based solely on AI analysis
  • Harass or defame individuals based on results
  • Use for unauthorized surveillance
  • Violate others' privacy rights

Known Limitations

Compression Artifacts

Heavily compressed images may produce less accurate results due to lost detail.

Low Resolution

Very low resolution images or videos may lack sufficient detail for accurate detection.

Novel Techniques

New deepfake generation methods may not be detected until models are updated.

Edge Cases

Unusual lighting, angles, or artistic styles may affect accuracy.
